🐾 Cats Are Obligate Carnivores
Cats must eat meat to survive. Their bodies cannot produce taurine on their own.

🐾 Taurine Is Important
Without enough taurine, cats can develop heart and eye problems.

🐾 Wet Food vs Dry Food
Wet food helps hydration. Dry food helps reduce plaque. Mixing both is best.

🐾 Small Frequent Meals
Feed 2–3 times a day in small portions.

🐾 Dangerous Human Foods
Never give chocolate, onion, garlic, grapes, caffeine, cooked bones.
