Pawsitive Care Guidelines

  • Always provide fresh drinking water.
  • Brush fur regularly to maintain a shiny coat.
  • Schedule routine veterinary checkups.
  • Keep the litter box clean daily.
  • Provide toys and playtime for mental health.
  • Create a warm sleeping space.
  • Feed balanced meals at fixed times.
  • Observe behavior changes carefully.
  • Give love and gentle affection daily.
